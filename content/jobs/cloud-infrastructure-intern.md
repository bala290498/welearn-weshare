## Job Description

We are looking for enthusiastic students or recent graduates to join our Cloud Infrastructure team as interns. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience with cloud technologies, work on real-world projects, and learn from experienced professionals.

## Key Responsibilities

- Assist in managing and monitoring cloud infrastructure
- Learn and work with AWS services (EC2, S3, Lambda, etc.)
- Help with infrastructure automation and scripting
- Participate in team meetings and knowledge sharing sessions
- Document processes and procedures
- Support the team with day-to-day operations
- Work on assigned projects and tasks

## Required Skills & Qualifications

- Currently pursuing Computer Science, IT, or related field
- Basic knowledge of cloud computing concepts
- Familiarity with Linux operating systems
- Basic programming skills (Python preferred)
- Strong willingness to learn
- Good communication skills
- Available for 6 months internship duration
- Available for full-time internship (40 hours/week)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Previous internship or project experience
- AWS certifications (AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner)
- Knowledge of containerization (Docker)
- Experience with version control (Git)
- Understanding of networking basics

## What We Offer

- Hands-on experience with cloud infrastructure
- Mentorship from experienced cloud engineers
- Opportunity to work on real-world projects
- Certificate of completion
- Potential for full-time employment after internship
- Stipend (Not Disclosed)
- Flexible learning environment

## About CloudTech

CloudTech is a cloud infrastructure specialist company with 100-500 employees, helping businesses migrate to and manage cloud solutions. We provide a supportive learning environment for interns to grow their skills.

## Application Process

To apply for this internship, please send your resume, academic transcripts, and a brief cover letter explaining your interest in cloud infrastructure to the email address provided. We review applications on a rolling basis.
