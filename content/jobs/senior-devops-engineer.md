## Job Description

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior DevOps Engineer to join our dynamic team. You will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining our cloud infrastructure, ensuring high availability, scalability, and security of our systems.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement scalable cloud infrastructure solutions
- Automate deployment processes using CI/CD pipelines
- Monitor and optimize system performance and reliability
- Collaborate with development teams to improve application architecture
- Implement security best practices and compliance requirements
- Troubleshoot and resolve infrastructure issues
- Document infrastructure and deployment processes

## Required Skills & Qualifications

- 5+ years of hands-on experience in DevOps/Infrastructure engineering
- Strong knowledge of containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions)
- Proficiency in cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Strong scripting skills (Bash, Python, or similar)
- Knowledge of monitoring and logging tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack)
- Understanding of networking, security, and compliance
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with microservices architecture
- Knowledge of service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
- Experience with GitOps practices
- Cloud certifications (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with database administration
